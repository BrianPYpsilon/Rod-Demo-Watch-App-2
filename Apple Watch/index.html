Glances

A glance is a read-only page which is shown after swiping up on your Watch, but only in case you've enabled glances in the stock 'Apple Watch' app. These buttons push differt kinds of glances to your watch, our default is 'Map'.

App pages

A page is shown when the user taps a glance, or launches the app itself on the Watch.
